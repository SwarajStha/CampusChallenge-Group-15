"  • 0.20 to 0.59 = Moderate buy signal (somewhat confident the stock will rise).\n"

"  • -0.01 to -0.19 = Weak sell signal (slight negative tilt).\n"
"  • -0.20 to -0.59 = Moderate sell signal (somewhat confident the stock will fall).\n"

"Line 2: one complete sentence (under 100 characters) briefly explaining the reason for your decision.\n"

"   Moderate negative (-0.2 to -0.6): Slight miss, cautious outlook, lawsuit risk, executive turmoil, macro headwind directly stated.\n"
"   Mixed/uncertain: Weigh which side dominates; keep |score| smaller (0.05–0.25) if ambiguous.\n"
"4) Calibration: Prefer smaller magnitudes unless the title clearly indicates a large surprise. Avoid bias. If uncertain, choose a small magnitude (±0.05 to ±0.15).\n"
